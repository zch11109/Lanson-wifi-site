How to Activate the Wi-Fi Profile on iPhone

Please follow these steps carefully to activate your Wi-Fi connection.

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. At the top of Settings, tap Profile Downloaded.
  3. Tap Install in the top right corner.
  4. Enter your device passcode if prompted.
  5. Tap Install again to confirm.
  6. Once installed, your device will automatically connect to the Wi-Fi network.
